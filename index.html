About Bellisa Court

Bellisa Court is located in one of Penang's prime areas. With Paragon Mall and Gurney Plaza located right across the street, having access to shopping, entertainment, and modern amenities is just a few minutes walk away. Some of the best local delicacies can also be found across the street.

After a long day's shopping, retreat back to Belisa Court's private and peaceful living with its old-world charm and grandeur of a bygone era, accented by its charming courtyard pool gardens. Stay at Bellisa Court to experience what the old and new has to offer.
